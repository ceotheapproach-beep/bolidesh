About Bolidesh Foundation

Bolidesh Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing free, reliable, and India-centric knowledge resources. Inspired by the depth of Wikipedia and the dynamism of BBC News, we integrate encyclopedia articles, historical archives, statistical data, news reports, content quality indexes, and corporate tools into a single, accessible platform.

Our mission is to empower individuals and communities across India by offering high-quality, multilingual resources that preserve cultural heritage, inform through data, and support professional growth. All content is freely available under Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike, ensuring open access to knowledge.

Founded in 2023, Bolidesh Foundation operates with a commitment to neutrality, accuracy, and inclusivity, serving as a trusted hub for India's diverse knowledge needs.
