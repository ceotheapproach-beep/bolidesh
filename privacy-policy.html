Privacy Policy

At Bolidesh Foundation, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices for handling data when you visit bolidesh.in or engage with our services, in compliance with India’s Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, and other applicable regulations.

1. Introduction

Bolidesh Foundation, an independent non-profit organization dedicated to preserving cultural heritage and fostering community development, values your trust. This Privacy Policy explains our commitment to safeguarding your personal information and ensuring that your experience on our website is secure, transparent, and compliant with data protection laws. Whether you’re exploring our cultural archives, subscribing to our newsletter, or making a donation, we prioritize your privacy and aim to provide clear information about how your data is handled.

This policy applies to all users of bolidesh.in, including visitors, donors, newsletter subscribers, and participants in our programs. By using our website or services, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at help.bolideshfoundation@gmail.com or +91 95557 54494.

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services, ensure a seamless user experience, and fulfill our mission of cultural preservation and community empowerment. The information we collect falls into the following categories:

2.1 Personal Information

Personal information is data that can identify you as an individual. We may collect personal information when you voluntarily provide it through our website or services, including:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address provided when you fill out contact forms, subscribe to newsletters, or make donations.
  • Donation Details: Payment information, such as credit card details or bank account information, processed securely through trusted third-party payment gateways.
  • User-Generated Content: Comments, feedback, or submissions you provide through forms, surveys, or interactive features on our website.
  • Account Information: If you create an account for accessing exclusive content or services, we may collect login credentials and profile information.

2.2 Non-Personal Information

Non-personal information does not directly identify you but helps us understand how users interact with our website. This includes:

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, and internet service provider.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on the website, click patterns, and navigation behavior.
  • Geolocation Data: Approximate location based on your IP address to tailor content or comply with regional regulations.

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to enhance your experience and collect data about website usage. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us:

  • Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Analyze website performance and user behavior.
  • Deliver personalized content, such as tailored newsletters or recommendations.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may impact the functionality of certain website features.

2.4 Third-Party Data

We may receive information from third-party platforms, such as social media (e.g., Facebook, Twitter, Instagram) or analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics), when you interact with our content on those platforms. This data is subject to the privacy policies of those third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to support our mission, improve our services, and ensure a secure and engaging user experience. The primary purposes include:

  • Service Delivery: To provide access to our website, cultural archives, and educational resources.
  • Communication: To respond to inquiries, process donations, and send newsletters or updates (with your consent).
  • Website Improvement: To analyze user behavior and optimize website performance, content, and functionality.
  • Fundraising and Donations: To process and acknowledge donations, ensuring secure transactions through trusted payment processors.
  • Marketing and Outreach: To send promotional materials, event invitations, or updates about our cultural preservation initiatives (only with your explicit consent).
  • Security and Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent technical issues, security breaches, or fraudulent activities.
  • Legal Compliance: To meet regulatory requirements under applicable laws, such as the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

We ensure that all data usage aligns with the principles of transparency, necessity, and user consent, as outlined in Indian data protection regulations.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Bolidesh Foundation does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. However, we may share your data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party providers who assist with website hosting, payment processing, analytics, or marketing (e.g., Google Analytics, payment gateways). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.
  • Legal Obligations: When required by law, such as in response to court orders, government requests, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Bolidesh Foundation, our users, or the public.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred to a successor entity, with continued commitment to privacy protection.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with partners or affiliates for specific purposes (e.g., collaborative cultural projects) if you provide explicit consent.

We ensure that any data sharing complies with applicable laws and is conducted with strict safeguards to protect your privacy.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies and similar technologies are integral to providing a seamless and personalized user experience. We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly, such as enabling navigation and secure transactions.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect data on how users interact with the website to improve performance and functionality.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences, such as language settings or login details.
  • Analytics Cookies: Track website usage and trends to help us optimize content and user experience.

You can control cookie settings through your browser or our cookie consent tool (if implemented). Disabling cookies may limit certain website features. For more information, refer to our Cookie Policy.

6. Data Security

We implement robust administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption: Using SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission.
  • Access Controls: Restricting access to personal data to authorized personnel only.
  • Regular Audits: Conducting security assessments to identify and address vulnerabilities.
  • Secure Storage: Storing data on secure servers with advanced firewalls and intrusion detection systems.

While we strive to maintain the highest security standards, no system is entirely immune to risks. We encourage users to protect their accounts by using strong passwords and avoiding sharing sensitive information.

7. Your Data Protection Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, and other applicable laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: Request details about the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: Request updates or corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Deletion: Request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal or operational requirements.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitations on how we process your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time, where applicable.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of marketing communications by clicking “unsubscribe” in emails or contacting us.

To exercise these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at help.bolideshfoundation@gmail.com. We will respond to requests within the timelines specified by applicable laws (typically 30 days).

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, such as social media platforms, payment processors, or partner organizations. Bolidesh Foundation is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

9. Children’s Privacy

Bolidesh Foundation is committed to protecting the privacy of children. Our website is not intended for users under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will promptly delete it. Parents or guardians with concerns about children’s data are encouraged to contact us immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

While our website is primarily designed for users in India, it is accessible globally. If you access bolidesh.in from outside India, your data may be processed in India or other jurisdictions where our service providers operate. We ensure that international data transfers comply with applicable data protection laws, including the use of standard contractual clauses or other safeguards as required.

11. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or to comply with legal, regulatory, or operational requirements. For example:

  • Donation records may be retained for tax and auditing purposes as required by law.
  • Newsletter subscription data is retained until you unsubscribe.
  • Usage data is anonymized and retained for analytics purposes.

Once data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymized in accordance with best practices.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

13. Legal Compliance

Bolidesh Foundation is committed to complying with applicable data protection laws, including the Information Technology Act, 2000, and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. We adhere to principles of transparency, accountability, and user empowerment, ensuring that our data practices align with regulatory standards and best practices.

If you have questions, concerns, or complaints about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact our Data Protection Officer:

  • Phone: +91 95557 54494

15. Additional Policies

Certain activities, such as donations, contests, or user-generated content submissions, may be subject to additional terms or policies. These will be clearly communicated at the point of interaction. We recommend reviewing any applicable terms before participating.

16. Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of September 28, 2025. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Bolidesh Foundation, we are dedicated to protecting your personal information and fostering trust through transparency and accountability. Our Privacy Policy is designed to provide clarity on how we handle your data while ensuring compliance with Indian and international data protection laws. We value your engagement with our mission to preserve cultural heritage and are committed to safeguarding your privacy every step of the way.
