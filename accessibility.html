Accessibility Statement

Bolidesh Foundation is committed to ensuring that our website, bolidesh.in, is accessible to all users, including individuals with disabilities.

Our Commitment

We strive to meet or exceed the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. Our goal is to make our content usable, understandable, and navigable for everyone, regardless of physical, cognitive, or technological challenges.

Accessibility Features

  • Keyboard Navigation: All interactive elements, menus, and links are navigable using a keyboard.
  • Screen Reader Compatibility: The website is compatible with screen readers, including appropriate use of ARIA labels and semantic HTML tags.
  • Color Contrast: Text and background colors meet recommended contrast ratios to ensure readability.
  • Alternative Text: All meaningful images include descriptive alt text for screen readers.
  • Resizable Text: Users can adjust font sizes without breaking the layout.
  • Accessible Forms: All forms include labels and error messages accessible to assistive technologies.
  • Responsive Design: The website adapts to different screen sizes and devices, including mobile, tablet, and desktop.

Limitations

While we work to make our website fully accessible, there may be some limitations due to third-party content, embedded media, or older archived materials. Bolidesh Foundation continually reviews and updates the site to minimize accessibility barriers.

Compatibility

Bolidesh.in is designed to work with current web browsers, including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and mobile browsers. We also ensure that assistive technologies, such as screen readers, magnifiers, and voice control software, can interact with the site effectively.

Ongoing Improvements

We regularly evaluate and update our website to improve accessibility. This includes:

  • Updating HTML and CSS for better semantic structure.
  • Ensuring new content adheres to accessibility guidelines.
  • Providing training to content creators on inclusive design.
  • Monitoring user feedback and implementing solutions promptly.

Legal and Compliance

Our accessibility statement reflects our commitment to inclusivity but does not alter our legal obligations or terms of service. Bolidesh Foundation strives to comply with applicable national accessibility laws and international best practices.
