About Araria District

Araria district, located in northeastern Bihar, is known for its agriculture, vibrant communities, and cross-border trade with Nepal. Key crops include rice, wheat, and maize. The district faces seasonal floods but is actively engaged in development initiatives and community programs to uplift local residents.
