Privacy Policy

At Bolidesh Foundation, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you visit bolidesh.in and use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect several types of information from our visitors, including but not limited to:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, phone number, or any other details you voluntarily provide when subscribing to newsletters, filling out contact forms, or donating.
  • Non-Personal Information: This includes your IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, pages viewed, and navigation patterns on our website.
  • Cookies and Tracking Data: We use cookies, pixels, and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and collect usage information.
  • Third-Party Data: If you interact with our social media pages or third-party services integrated with our website, we may receive data from those platforms in accordance with their privacy policies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our website and services.
  • To respond to inquiries, comments, or requests.
  • To send newsletters, updates, and promotional materials if you have opted in.
  • To process donations and facilitate fundraising campaigns.
  • To monitor and analyze website usage, trends, and traffic patterns.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or security breaches.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your data in limited circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers and partners who assist us in operating our website, conducting our programs, or processing donations.
  • In response to legal requirements, such as court orders, governmental requests, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Bolidesh Foundation, our users, or others.
  • During a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction, with continued commitment to privacy protection.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Bolidesh Foundation uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Enhance user experience and site functionality.
  • Understand user behavior and analyze traffic trends.
  • Provide targeted content, advertisements, or notifications.

You may manage or disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some features of the website may not function properly without cookies.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your personal information. However, please note that no transmission or storage of data is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access the information we hold about you.
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal or operational requirements.
  • The right to opt-out of receiving marketing communications.
  • The right to withdraw consent to data processing where applicable.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at help.bolideshfoundation@gmail.com.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to third-party websites for informational purposes. Bolidesh Foundation is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these websites. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites they visit.

8. Children's Privacy

Bolidesh Foundation does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly. Parents and guardians are encouraged to monitor online activity and contact us regarding any concerns.

9. International Users

Our website is primarily intended for users in India, but users from other countries may access it. By using our website, you acknowledge that your data may be processed in India and other jurisdictions. We take steps to ensure compliance with applicable data protection laws for international users.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

Bolidesh Foundation may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in practices, technology, or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of our website constitutes acceptance of any updates or modifications.

11. Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or complaints regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: help.bolideshfoundation@gmail.com
  • Phone: +91 95557 54494

12. Legal Compliance

Bolidesh Foundation complies with applicable privacy and data protection laws, including the Information Technology Act, 2000, and relevant Indian regulations. We take our obligations seriously and strive to maintain transparency, accountability, and best practices in handling personal information.

13. Retention of Data

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or to comply with legal, regulatory, or internal policy requirements. Once data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

14. Your Consent

By using bolidesh.in, you consent to the collection, use, and sharing of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please refrain from using the website or submitting personal information.

15. Additional Policies

Additional terms related to donations, subscriptions, contests, or user-generated content may include separate privacy or usage policies. We recommend reviewing those as applicable.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of September 2025. Any updates or revisions will be posted on this page with the revised effective date.

Bolidesh Foundation is committed to transparency, accountability, and the protection of your personal information. Your trust is valuable to us, and we strive to ensure that your experience on bolidesh.in is safe, secure, and respectful of your privacy.
