Support Bolidesh Foundation

Your generous contribution helps preserve India’s cultural heritage, empower communities, and drive sustainable initiatives for a brighter future.

Why Your Donation Matters

At Bolidesh Foundation, we are committed to preserving India’s rich cultural heritage while empowering communities through education, research, and sustainable development. Your donation fuels our mission to safeguard traditions, support artisans, and make cultural knowledge accessible to all. Every contribution, no matter the size, creates a lasting impact on communities across India and beyond.

By donating, you become a partner in our journey to protect ancient manuscripts, revive traditional crafts, and empower the next generation with cultural pride. Join us in ensuring that India’s heritage remains vibrant for future generations.

The Impact of Your Donation

Your support directly contributes to transformative initiatives that preserve cultural heritage and uplift communities. Here’s how your donation makes a difference:

  • Preserving Heritage: Fund the digitization of cultural artifacts, oral histories, and traditional practices, ensuring they are accessible worldwide.
  • Empowering Communities: Support education programs and workshops that teach traditional crafts and foster cultural pride among youth and adults.
  • Sustainable Development: Provide resources for artisans and performers to create sustainable livelihoods through their cultural skills.
  • Global Advocacy: Enable partnerships with international organizations to protect cultural heritage from threats like urbanization and climate change.

With your help, we’ve already digitized over 15,000 artifacts, empowered 75,000 individuals, and supported 2,000 artisans. Your donation will help us expand this impact.

How to Donate

We’ve made donating simple and secure through PhonePe, a trusted payment platform. Follow these steps to contribute:

  1. Open the PhonePe app on your smartphone.
  2. Tap the “Scan” option to scan the QR code below.
  3. Enter the amount you wish to donate and confirm the payment.
  4. You’ll receive a confirmation of your donation, and we’ll follow up with a thank-you note and impact updates.

Important: Replace YOUR_PHONEPE_QR_CODE_URL with the actual URL of your PhonePe QR code image. Ensure the image is hosted securely (e.g., on a cloud service like Google Drive, Dropbox, or your website) and accessible publicly.

If you encounter issues or have questions, contact us at help.bolidesh@gmail.com or WhatsApp.
