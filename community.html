Join Our Community

Bolidesh Foundation thrives on the contributions of our vibrant community. Join us to create, edit, and share India-centric knowledge across encyclopedia articles, archives, statistics, news, indexes, and corporate resources.

Ways to Contribute

  • Content Creation: Write or edit articles to expand our knowledge base.
  • Research: Provide data or historical insights for accuracy.
  • Translation: Help make content accessible in multiple Indian languages.
  • Feedback: Share suggestions to improve our platform.

To get started, email us at community@bolidesh.in or join our forums on Twitter and Facebook.

About Bolidesh

Bolidesh Foundation is a non-profit dedicated to free knowledge for India, blending encyclopedic reliability with news timeliness.
